5、What are the principles for the selection and voting of Content Review Panel?

The content review panel is the content management agency of FACTS. Members of the content review panel are produced in accordance with the principles of fairness and transparency. Each content review panel member has a lower limit. Number of people required. There are several content review panels on an active content platform. Each piece of content generated in a simultaneous work network requires a fact review vote of the content review panel to determine whether it can be marked as a FACTS content.

Eligibility criteria and voting principles:

1、 Users who are active within a certain period of time hold a certain number of FACTS Tokens and willing to pledge a certain FACTS Tokens before voting (users are active within a certain period of time refer to users who take part in content production, review and participate other interactions);

2、 The voting members of content review panel are drawn at random from user pool continuously until a certain number of people among them have completed the judgment. At the same time, refer to the professional domain matching relationship between the user and the content being voted.If the corresponding number of votes has not been completed within the specified time, it will continue to drawn candidates from the user pool;

3、 The content review panel obtains FACTS Tokens. The content review panel follows their rules to vote. The party with the voting proportion of more than 60% will obtain the tokens. In the judging period, if they are reported by users and the report is passed by new term content review panel, then their original token rewards will be deducted and these tokens will be given to another party.

The pledged FACTS Tokens cannot be transferred or segmented. Only after the party exits the content review panel and all voting contents that this party involves have gone through the appeal period, the FACTS Tokens can be unfrozen.If the party needs enforcement unfrozen, then among rewards that this party has obtained by taking part in voting, the corresponding review rewards in appeal period will be destroyed.

6、What is the Appeal period？

A piece of content will obtain rewards after the expiry of evaluation period. In order to have enough time to identify infringement, trustfulness and other behaviors, the authors shall obtain the rewards after a certain period of locking-up.

In order to prevent malicious authors from releasing infringement or violation contents, users can launch a new term content review panel by initiating content reporting complaints. This kind of contents can be frozen according to the voting result of content review panel , which the frozen contents will not be able to continue to receive evaluation and the authors will fail to obtain the revenue that can be obtained under normal conditions as well. During the appeal period, content authors also have the right for arbitration and appeal, which ensures that all users are able to be treated fairly.

Time is one of the most important indexes for verifying content trustfulness, too. FACTS adds a relatively long appeal period to ensure there are sufficient evidences to prove the trustfulness of contents before they enter the content pool.

7、What is the technical architecture of FACTS?

This project will develop a set of content public chains based on the existing blockchain mature public chain and distributed file storage projects. The entire project will be divided into three levels: the base chain layer, the transaction layer, the content management layer, the protocol layer, and the application layer. The requirements for each level of the system's infrastructure, content management, and content applications will be clearly defined.

At the infrastructure level, core operations such as currency trading, content storage and packaging of blockchain cores will be established.

At the level of content management, FACTS Graph and FACTS Index will be established based on artificial intelligence algorithms, and high-quality content-driven logic will be established using Proof of FACTS (PoF).

At the content use level, various business systems and content ecological applications will be implemented to ensure the flow of content throughout the ecosystem.
